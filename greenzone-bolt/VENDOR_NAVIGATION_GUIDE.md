# GreenZone Vendor Navigation Guide

## 🗺️ Map Features & Vendor Dashboard Navigation

### MAP INTEGRATION

The platform now includes interactive map functionality for customers to discover businesses.

#### **Map Locations:**

1. **City Pages** (`/dispensaries/[city]`)
   - Toggle between List View and Map View
   - Click the map icon in the top-right toolbar
   - View all businesses in the city with interactive markers
   - Click markers to view business details

2. **Map Component Features:**
   - Shows up to 5 businesses with numbered markers
   - Clickable markers navigate to business profiles
   - Map legend showing marker meanings
   - Coordinates display for location verification
   - Responsive design works on mobile and desktop

#### **How Customers Use the Map:**

```
1. Visit /dispensaries/los-angeles-ca
2. Click the map icon (toggle in search bar)
3. See all dispensaries plotted on map
4. Click any green marker to view that business
5. Switch back to list view anytime
```

### **2. Business Profile Management**

**Location:** `/vendor/profile`

**How to Edit Your Profile:**

1. Click "Business Profile" in the sidebar
2. Update fields:
   - Business name
   - Description
   - Business type
   - Phone number
   - Website
   - Email
   - Street address
   - City, State, ZIP
   - Hours of operation (each day)
3. Upload photos/videos in the media section
4. Click "Save Changes"

**What Gets Updated:**
- Your public listing page
- Search results appearance
- Customer-facing information

---

### **3. Deals Management**

**Location:** `/vendor/deals`

**Creating a New Deal:**

1. Click "Deals & Offers" in sidebar
2. Click "Create New Deal" button
3. Fill in:
   - Deal title (e.g., "20% Off Edibles")
   - Description
   - Category (Flower, Edibles, Vapes, etc.)
   - Start date
   - End date
4. Upload deal image
5. Click "Publish Deal"

**Tracking Deal Performance:**

Each deal shows:
- Views: How many people saw it
- Clicks: How many people clicked
- CTR: Click-through rate percentage
- Status: Active, Scheduled, or Expired

**Upgrading to Sponsored:**
- Click "Upgrade to Sponsored" on any active deal
- Get 10x more visibility
- Appears in homepage deal carousel
- Featured in city pages

---

### **4. Understanding Sidebar Navigation**

**Active Page Highlighting:**

**Badge Indicators:**

**Business Card at Top:**
